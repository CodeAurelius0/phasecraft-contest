AskNestlé

Nutrition Information Simplified

Nestlé India's corporate service pillar website that aims to deliver authentic nutrition information to families.

AskNestlé is a platform that offers simplified nutrition information for varied needs with the help of Nestlé experts.

Available in English, Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu and Bengali, it provides access to healthy recipes, detailed nutrition information, and tools like a customized meal planner, growth tracker, and Immuno Scale.

The Nestlé Journey

From a humble beginning in Switzerland to nourishing millions of Indian families — our story spans over 150 years.

1866

The Beginning

Henri Nestlé creates a breakthrough infant food, saving the life of a neighbor's child. The Nestlé company is founded in Vevey, Switzerland.

1905

Global Expansion

Nestlé merges with the Anglo-Swiss Condensed Milk Company, expanding its product portfolio and geographical reach across continents.

1961

Nestlé India

Nestlé establishes its first factory in India at Moga, Punjab, transforming the Indian food industry and empowering local dairy farmers.

2018

AskNestlé Launches

AskNestlé is born as a digital platform to provide expert-backed nutrition guidance, healthy recipes, and personalized tools for Indian families.
